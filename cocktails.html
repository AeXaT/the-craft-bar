Cocktail Recipes

From timeless classics to modern craft creations

Old Fashioned

60 ml Bourbon, sugar cube, 3-4 dash of Angostura bitters. Stir with ice, strain into a rocks glass with big cube, garnish with orange peel.

Whiskey

Negroni

30ml gin, 30ml Campari, 30ml sweet vermouth. Stir with ice, strain over a large cube, garnish with orange peel.

Gin

Margarita

60ml tequila, 30ml triple sec, 30ml fresh lime juice. Shake with ice, strain into a salt-rimmed glass.

Tequila

Daiquiri

60ml white rum, 30ml fresh lime juice, 10ml simple syrup. Shake hard with ice and double strain.

Rum

Moscow Mule

60ml vodka, 25ml lime juice, top with ginger beer. Serve in a copper mug over ice with a lime wedge.

Vodka

Espresso Martini

60ml vodka, 30ml espresso, 25ml coffee liqueur, 1 bar spoon simple syrup. Shake vigorously and strain.
