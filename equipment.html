Essential Bar Tools

Every professional bartender relies on quality tools. Here's what you need to stock your bar.

Cocktail Shaker

The cobbler or Boston shaker — your most essential tool. Used for shaking spirits with ice to chill and dilute. A good shaker has a tight seal and comfortable grip.

Jigger

Precision is everything in mixology. A double jigger (25ml / 50ml) ensures consistent, well-balanced cocktails every time. Never free-pour in a professional setting.

Bar Spoon

A long-handled spoon used for stirring cocktails. Stirred drinks like the Negroni or Manhattan require gentle movement to chill without over-diluting.

Muddler

Used to crush fruits, herbs, and sugar in the bottom of a glass. Essential for Mojitos, Old Fashioneds, and any drink requiring fresh muddled ingredients.

Strainer

A Hawthorne strainer fits over the shaker and filters out ice when pouring. A fine mesh strainer is used for double-straining to remove small ice chips.

Citrus Juicer

Fresh juice is non-negotiable in craft cocktails. A hand juicer or Mexican elbow press extracts maximum juice quickly. Never use bottled citrus in serious cocktails.

Paring Knife & Board

For cutting garnishes — citrus twists, wedges, wheels. A sharp knife and dedicated cutting board keep prep fast and clean behind the bar.
